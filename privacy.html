Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 05, 2026

Welcome to LingoRise English (also known as EasyEnglish). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

A. Authentication Data

If you choose to sign in using Google Sign-In, we collect basic profile information provided by Google to create your account and synchronize your progress across devices:

  • Email Address: To identify your account and allow data recovery.
  • Name: To display on your profile and the public leaderboard.
  • Profile Picture: To display on your profile.

You can also use the application in "Guest" mode without providing any personal information, but your progress will be stored locally on your device and may be lost if the app is uninstalled.

B. Application Data (Gameplay & Progress)

We collect data related to your learning progress to provide the core functionality of the app:

  • Words learned and vocabulary progress.
  • XP (Experience Points), Level, and current Streak.
  • Quiz results and Duel (Multiplayer) statistics.
  • Virtual currency balance (Coins) and inventory items (Themes, Frames).

C. Device and Usage Data

We generally collect information about your device to improve app stability and user experience:

  • Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers (Android ID, Advertising ID).
  • Crash logs and performance data via Firebase Crashlytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Cloud Syncing: To save your learning progress (XP, words) to Firebase Cloud Firestore so you can access it on multiple devices.
  • Leaderboard: To display your designated username and XP score on the global leaderboard, visible to other users.
  • Multiplayer (Duels): To match you with other players for real-time quizzes.
  • Advertising: To serve personalized ads (Google AdMob) that help keep the app free.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services that may collect and process data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Analytics, Crashlytics): Used for backend infrastructure, authentication, and tracking app stability.
    Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements, including Rewarded Ads (e.g., watch a video to earn coins). AdMob may use device identifiers (Advertising ID) to serve personalized ads.
    Google AdMob Privacy Policy

4. Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated data. If you wish to delete your data:

  1. If logged in, you can request deletion by contacting us at the email address below.
  2. Upon request, we will permanently remove your user record and progress from our Firebase database.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.
